Biriyani

Biryani is a beloved South Asian rice dish known for its incredible flavors. It combines fragrant basmati rice with a mix of tender meat, vegetables, and an array of aromatic spices. This delightful dish is cherished for its rich taste and is prepared in various regional styles.
